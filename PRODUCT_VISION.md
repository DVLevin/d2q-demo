## ğŸ¨ Design Principles

### 1. Confidence Through Clarity
- No jargon without explanation
- Every AI decision is explainable
- Confidence scores are visible, not hidden

### 2. Honesty Through Labeling
- Clear distinction between "working" and "planned"
- Demo mode is explicitly stated
- No fake interactivity that implies production readiness

### 3. Professionalism Through Restraint
- Clean, industrial aesthetic (matches manufacturing context)
- Minimal animations (not a consumer app)
- Data-forward design (numbers matter)

### 4. Trust Through Familiarity
- Their drawings, not stock images
- Their terminology (Fiche de ContrÃ´le, not "QC Sheet")
- Their language (French primary, English secondary)
